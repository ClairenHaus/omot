The bigger picture

Abuse does not end on its own.

The cycle of domestic violence is inter-generational. Children who witness abuse are significantly more likely to experience it as adults — either as victims or perpetrators. Breaking that cycle requires intervention, education, and sustained community support.

OMOT exists at that intervention point. Every survivor we reach is a cycle broken. Your gift makes that possible.
