Organizations

Become a Community Partner

OMOT's ability to serve survivors depends on strong relationships with organizations across both regions. If your organization works in healthcare, legal services, housing, education, or community support — we want to hear from you.

Referral Partners

Send survivors to OMOT, or receive warm referrals from us. We work with shelters, hospitals, schools, faith communities, and social service agencies.

Resource Partners

Offer in-kind support — space for workshops, printed materials, transportation resources, or professional services donated to our program participants.

Corporate Partners

Support OMOT through employee volunteer programs, sponsorships, or matching gift campaigns. We welcome businesses that want to invest in the communities they operate in.

Faith Communities

Host OMOT workshops, provide meeting space, or serve as a trusted referral point for congregation members experiencing abuse.

Pro bono support

Donate Your Skills

Some of the most impactful contributions are not financial — they are professional. If you have expertise to offer, OMOT has a place for you.

Legal

Attorneys who can assist survivors with protective orders, custody, immigration status, and housing rights.

Mental Health

Licensed counselors and therapists who can offer sessions or group support for program participants.

Financial

Financial coaches, CPAs, and credit counselors who can support our financial literacy programming.

Healthcare

Medical professionals who can provide information, referrals, or volunteer at OMOT events.

Education

Educators and trainers who can develop or deliver curriculum on abuse prevention and survivor empowerment.

Technology

Developers, designers, and tech professionals who can support OMOT's digital presence and tools.
