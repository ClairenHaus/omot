Est. 2015

Breaking the cycle. Building the future.

Owning My Own Truth, Inc. was founded by Tina and Jeania Robinson to meet the needs of men, women, and children experiencing physical, mental, and emotional abuse in underserved communities.

Our mission is to break the inter-generational cycle of abuse — empowering survivors to move from victimization to emotional health, independence, and community.
